STEPS TO COPY AND PASTE YOUR SIGNATURE TO YOUR EMAIL

  • Left click and drag over all your signature's content (everything should be highlighted blue).
  • Once all is highlighted blue hit CTRL + C.
  • Open gmail, click the settings icon at the top right next to your profile picture, click "See all settings".
  • Scroll down till you see "Signature" then click "create new" and name your signature.
  • Once named hit CTRL + V to paste in the signature into the box.
  • Scroll to bottom and hit save.
  • IMPORTANT: IF SIGNATURE DOESNT FORMAT CORRECTY TRY RE COPYING IT BUT STARTING CLOSER TO THE CENTER OF PAGE, IF ISSUE STILL OCCURS REACH OUT TO ME (CIERRA) AND I WILL HELP
